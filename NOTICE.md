Twitter Hide Ads is an independent LSPosed module and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by X Corp., Twitter, LSPosed, or their maintainers.

Version 1.0.0 contains a compatibility profile validated against X Android 12.7.1. X may rename or restructure internal classes in later releases. The module therefore fails open on unknown versions rather than applying an untested render-model mapping.
